Day 1: What is Motion? Setting the Stage

üîë What is Motion?

Motion is the change in position of an object with respect to time and relative to a reference frame.

üí°

Key Insight: Motion is always relative! A baseball may be moving at 95 mph relative to home plate, but stationary relative to the pitcher's hand during the wind-up.
