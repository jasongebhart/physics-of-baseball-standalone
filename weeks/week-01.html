Week 1: Introduction to the Physics of Motion

This week, we'll lay the foundation for our journey into the physics of baseball by exploring the fundamental concepts of motion.

Day 1: What is Motion? Setting the Stage

An introduction to the fundamental concepts of motion, setting the stage for the rest of the course.

Day 2: Displacement and Distance

Understanding the difference between total path traveled and the straight-line change in position.

Day 3: Velocity and Speed

Calculating the rate of change of position, with and without direction.

Day 4: Advanced Motion Analysis

Using graphs and data to analyze motion in more detail.

Day 5: Applications and Assessment

Applying the concepts of motion to real baseball scenarios and assessing understanding.
