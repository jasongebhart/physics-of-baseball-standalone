The v = ωr Connection

Linear-Angular Velocity Relationship

Relationship: v = ωr

v = linear velocity, ω = angular velocity, r = radius

Baseball Example:

ω = 8000°/s = 140 rad/s

r = 0.6 m (effective arm length)

v = 140 × 0.6 = 84 m/s ≈ 188 mph

Key Point: Longer effective radius = higher velocity!
