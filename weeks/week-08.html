Backspin (Fastball)

  • 2000-2600 RPM
  • Upward Magnus force
  • "Hop" or "rise" effect

Topspin (Curveball)

  • 2200-3000+ RPM
  • Downward Magnus force
  • Sharp downward break

Sidespin (Slider)

  • Variable RPM
  • Lateral Magnus force
  • Horizontal sweeping motion
